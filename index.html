Characters

Information

Blossom

She is the smartest member and self-proclaimed leader of the Powerpuff Girls, is admired for her intelligence and composed demeanor.

Bubbles

She is regarded as the group's weakest link, Bubbles' overemotional nature contrasts with her occasional bursts of independence and aggression.

Buttercup

She is a fun-loving tomboy persona belies her strategic prowess, often emerging as the problem-solver during tough battles.

Bliss

She is depicted as the long-lost older sister of Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup, created by Professor Utonium. Bliss possesses the ability to harness and manipulate energy, granting her a range of superpowers.

Professor Utonium

He is the scientist behind the creation of The Powerpuff Girls, serves as a central character in the series. He imparts knowledge in quantum physics and micro-nuclear fusion
